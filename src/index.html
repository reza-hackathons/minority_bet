Minority Bet Zoo

Connect

To make a bet:

  1. Connect your wallet.
  2. Select either the 'Batman' or the 'Joker' box as your bet.
  3. Stake some Ⓝ tokens.
  4. Hit the 'Bet' button.
  5. Wait for the payout!

Caveat

Remember that this is a minority bet system where the party with the lowest staked amount wins.
This is very counter-intuitive especially in that in order to win you should either play small or sacrifice large amounts to win a larger bag which is itself a big loss. So winning in this game requires complex strategies which often leads to big losses.
